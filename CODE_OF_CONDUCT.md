# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Our standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Avoid discriminatory, hateful, or harassing language.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and technical issues.

Reporting
- If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, contact a project maintainer (Wiper15) privately.
- Maintainers will review and respond promptly.

Consequences
- Violations may result in warnings, temporary removal from the project, or permanent ban depending on severity.

Thank you for helping build a friendly, inclusive community.
